About the Dataset

The "Videogame Sales Analysis" dataset is a treasure trove of information about video games from various platforms. It contains valuable insights to unravel the gaming world's mysteries. The dataset consists of essential columns that shed light on each game's characteristics and popularity. These columns include the game's name, the platform it runs on, its release year, genre, and the responsible publisher. Moreover, it provides comprehensive sales figures, indicating the number of units sold in North America, Europe, Japan, and other regions, as well as the total global sales. Additionally, the dataset offers a glimpse into the critical and user ratings, showcasing the overall reception and appreciation of each game. This comprehensive collection of data opens up a world of possibilities to explore and analyze the trends and patterns that shape the gaming industry.

The dataset contains the following columns to work on.

Field Description
Name The name of a video game.
Platform The platform (PC, PS4, XBox, etc.) for which a game is released.
Year The release year of a video game.
Genre The genre of a video game.
Publisher The publisher of a video game.
NA_Sales Approximately, the total number of units sold (in million) of a video game in North America.
EU_Sales Approximately, the total number of units sold (in million) of a video game in Europe.
JP_Sales Approximately, the total number of units sold (in million) of a video game in Japan.
Other_Sales Approximately, the total number of units sold (in million) of a video game in the rest of the world.
Global_Sales Approximately, the total number of units sold (in million) of a video game all over the world.
Critic_score Aggregate score compiled by Metacritic staff.
User_score Score by Metacritic's subscribers.
User_count Number of users who gave the user_score.
Developer Party responsible for creating the game.
Rating The ESRB ratings.
